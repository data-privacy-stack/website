The Data Privacy Stack is a community-led effort. This page outlines — at a high
level — how the project is organized and how decisions get made.

:::note[Work in progress]
Governance is being formalized. This page will be expanded as the structure
settles. Have input? [Open a discussion](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack).
:::

## Principles

- **Open by default.** Discussion and decisions happen in public.
- **Maintainer stewardship.** Each project has maintainers responsible for its
  direction, reviews, and releases.
- **Consensus-seeking.** Significant changes are proposed openly and discussed
  before adoption.

## Roles

- **Contributors** — anyone who opens issues, PRs, or participates in
  discussions.
- **Maintainers** — contributors with commit and review responsibility for a
  given project.
- **Org stewards** — maintainers who help coordinate across projects and manage
  the organization.

## Adding a project

New projects join the stack through an open proposal and discussion process, so
the community can weigh fit, scope, and maintenance commitment. See
[contributing](/community/contributing/) to start that conversation.
