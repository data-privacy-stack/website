The Data Privacy Stack uses **[Minimum Viable Governance (MVG)](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG)**
— a lightweight, repository-based governance model. Governance documents live in
the [`data-privacy-stack/MVG`](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG) repository.

## Mission

The organization exists to steward the **Presidio** open-source project by
providing transparent, consensus-based, and vendor-neutral governance that
supports its continued development, adoption, and sustainability.

## Two-tier structure

MVG defines two levels of governance:

### Organization — Technical Steering Committee (TSC)

The **TSC** makes decisions about overall direction and coordination across the
organization's projects. It is responsible for:

- Oversight of technical direction and roadmap
- Maintainer appointment and removal
- Governance policy development and enforcement
- Coordination of contributions across projects

See the [Charter](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG/blob/main/org-docs/CHARTER.md)
and [Steering Committee](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG/blob/main/org-docs/STEERING-COMMITTEE.md).

### Projects — Maintainers

Each project (e.g. Presidio) is run by its **Maintainers** through a lightweight,
**consensus-based** process. Consensus means general agreement among
participating maintainers after good-faith consideration of differing views — it
does not require unanimity. Maintainers:

- Review and approve contributions
- Manage pull requests and issue triage
- Keep the project secure, maintained, usable, and modern
- Participate in governance decisions

See the project [Governance](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG/blob/main/project-docs/GOVERNANCE.md)
and [Maintainers](https://github.com/data-privacy-stack/MVG/blob/main/project-docs/MAINTAINERS.md).

## Why MVG

MVG lets the community stand up real governance early — covering decision-making,
licensing, trademarks, and due process — without heavyweight overhead, and makes
it straightforward to grow into a formal foundation later if needed.
